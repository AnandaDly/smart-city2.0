Smart City
A smart city is an urban area that uses advanced technologies and data-driven solutions to enhance the quality of life for its residents, improve sustainability, and optimize urban operations. It integrates the Internet of Things (IoT), artificial intelligence (AI), and big data to create a more efficient, sustainable, and connected environment.

This project is made to introduce the smart city concept to a wide audience, especially the younger generation. With a cartoonish low-poly approach, we hope the smart city concept becomes easier to understand.

Made with: Three.js & Blender.
